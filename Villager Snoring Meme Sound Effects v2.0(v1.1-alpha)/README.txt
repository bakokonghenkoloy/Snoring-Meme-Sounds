=== Villager Snoring Meme Sound Effects v2.0 — RESOURCE PACK ===
by Rabbi S. Arlan

INSTALL: Drop this ZIP into .minecraft/resourcepacks/
         Then enable it in Options > Resource Packs

DO NOT UNZIP. Minecraft reads it as-is.

WHAT IT DOES:
- Overrides entity.villager.sleep with 5 unique meme snore sounds
- Each sound has 3 pitch variants (0.75 to 1.25) = 15 pool entries
- Minecraft picks randomly each time = no two snores sound the same
- Works on 1.21 through 26.1.2 and beyond — forever

NOTES:
- For PLAYER snoring, install the companion DATAPACK too
- Sound volume controlled by "Friendly Creatures" slider in audio settings
